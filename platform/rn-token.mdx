The $RN token is the ecosystem token of the Replicant Network. It represent the ownership stake in the network and it's role will be gradually increased as the network matures and becomes gradually decentralized. Users can expect the following features:

- Receive part of the revenue share of the whole platform
- Liquidity pairing of the model tokens
- Participate in deciding how the Replicant Network treasury is used
- Participate in the governance of the network
